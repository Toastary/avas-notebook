# Law Summary

+ https://www.congress.gov/crs-product/IF13006#:~:text=232.&text=Under%20Sec.,also%20self%2Dinitiate%20an%20investigation.
	+ Section 232 of the Trade Expansion Act of 1962
		+ According to Congress, the head of any department or agency, or any "interested party" may request that the Secretary of Commerce investigate the effects of a specific import on U.S. national security. The Commerce Secretary may also self-initiate an investigation
