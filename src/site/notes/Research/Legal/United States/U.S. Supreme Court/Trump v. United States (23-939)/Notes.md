## Notes

+ The President (both former, and current) of the [[Research/Library/Geography/United States\|United States]] is granted "absolute immunity" from their core official acts
	+ The President can't be federally prosecuted for their acts under office according to the Supreme Court's majority decision
		+ This ruling has protected [[Research/Library/People/Donald Trump\|Donald Trump]] from any federal prosecution relating to the mar-a-lago classified documents case, and the January 6 case which was brought up by [[Research/Library/People/Jack Smith\|Jack Smith]]
