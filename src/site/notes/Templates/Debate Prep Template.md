# Topic

- What exactly is being debated?
- Define key terms clearly
# My Position

- Clear thesis (1–2 sentences max)
- Is this moral, constitutional, economic, or practical argument?
# First Core Argument

- Claim
- Evidence
- Explanation (why the evidence proves the claim)
- Impact (why it matters)
# Second Core Argument

- Claim
- Evidence
- Explanation
- Impact
# Third Core Argument (Optional)

- Claim
- Evidence
- Explanation
- Impact
# Anticipated Counterarguments

- What will the other side say?
- What evidence will they use?
# My Rebuttals

- Direct response to each counterargument
- Show logical flaw, missing context, or weak evidence
- Flip the impact if possible
# Constitutional/Legal Angle (If Relevant)

- What branch has authority?
- Is this delegated power?
- Does precedent support my side?
# Ethical Framing

- Who benefits?
- Who is harmed?
- Is this consistent with democratic principles?
# Economic/Practical Consequences

- Short-term effects
- Long-term effects
- Unintended consequences
# Closing Statement Strategy

- Restate thesis
- Emphasize strongest impact
- Leave opponent defending something uncomfortable
