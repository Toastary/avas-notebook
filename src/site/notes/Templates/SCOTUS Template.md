# Relevance

+ Why this case matters today
+ Who it affects (states, federal gov, individuals, industries, etc.)
+ Political/constitutional impact
# Decision Summary

+ Who won?
+ Vote split (example, 6-3, or 5-4)
+ Who wrote the majority opinion?
# Major Points of Decisions

- Core constitutional reasoning used by the majority
- What clause/amendment/statute they interpreted
- Legal tests or standards applied
- Precedents relied on
- How they justified their interpretation
- Limits the Court placed (if any)
# Major Points of Dissent

- Main disagreements with the majority
- Alternative constitutional interpretation
- Warnings about consequences
- Claims about judicial overreach or restraint
- Precedents they believe were misused or ignored
# Questions for Debates

- Did the Court interpret or effectively create law?
- Did this expand or limit federal power?
- Was precedent respected properly?
- Should Congress respond legislatively?
- What are the long-term democratic impacts?
# Questions for me to Research

- What historical cases relate to this one?
- What did lower courts rule before this?
- How scholars are reacting
- What constitutional clause is most disputed?
- What real-world data might test the ruling’s impact?
