# Law Summary
___
- Full name of the statute
- Public Law number (if relevant)
- Year enacted
# Relevance
___
- Why this law matters today
- Who it affects (individuals, businesses, states, federal agencies)
- Political / constitutional significance
# Constitutional Authority
___
- What constitutional power Congress relied on
    - Commerce Clause?    
    - Taxing & Spending Clause?        
    - War Powers?        
    - Necessary & Proper Clause?        
- Any federalism concerns?
# Purpose of the Law
___
- What problem was Congress trying to solve?
- Historical context (economic crisis, war, social issue, etc.)
# Powers Granted
___
- What authority does the law give?
- Which branch or agency receives that power?
- Any deadlines, procedures, or required findings?
# Limitations / Restrictions
___
- Explicit limits in the statute
- Procedural safeguards
- Congressional oversight requirements
- Judicial review provisions
# Enforcement Mechanism
___
- Which agency enforces it?
- Civil penalties? Criminal penalties?
- Private right of action?
# Historical Use
___
- How has it been used by Presidents or agencies?
- Any expansions beyond original intent?
- Major controversies?
# Major Court Interpretations
___
- Key Supreme Court cases interpreting it
- Has it been narrowed or expanded by courts?
- Any parts struck down as unconstitutional?
# Debate Questions
___
- Is the delegation too broad?
- Does it shift power between branches?
- Has it been abused or stretched?
# Questions for Me to Research
___
- Legislative history
- Dissenting congressional views
- Comparative laws in other countries
- Economic / social data on effectiveness
